# Civil War Stories - Project Specification

## Project Overview
- **Project Name:** Civil War Stories
- **Type:** Children's educational website with interactive AI characters + digital book store
- **Core Functionality:** Sell a children's Civil War storybook while offering interactive chat experiences with historical figures from the book
- **Target Users:** Parents, educators, kids ages 6-12

## The Book
**Title:** "Voices of the War: A Child's Journey Through the Civil War"

A children's book told through real historical documents — letters, diary entries, and speeches from actual people who lived through the Civil War. No fictional characters.

**Featured Figures:**
- Harriet Tubman (former enslaved, Underground Railroad conductor)
- Abraham Lincoln (President)
- Clara Barton (nurse, founded Red Cross)
- James Longstreet (Confederate general - balanced perspective)
- A young Union soldier (based on real letters)
- An enslaved person's journey to freedom

**Format:** 20-page digital PDF ($4.99)

## The Website

### Pages
1. **Home/Landing** — Hook visitors, explain the concept, preview the book
2. **Book Page** — Purchase the PDF ($4.99)
3. **Chat with History** — Interactive chatbots of the book's characters

### Interactive Characters (Chat)
Users can chat with AI-powered versions of the historical figures:
- Ask questions about their experiences
- Learn history through conversation
- Kid-friendly, educational, accurate

### Tech Stack
- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- Vercel deployment
- Built-in AI chat (no external API needed — I am the AI)

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Vintage/storybook — warm sepia tones, aged paper textures
- **Colors:** Deep navy, burgundy, cream, gold accents
- **Typography:** Serif for story sections, clean sans-serif for UI
- **Vibe:** Educational, respectful, engaging for kids

- [ ] Landing page loads with book preview
- [ ] Book purchase link works (Gumroad/standalone)
- [ ] At least 3 interactive character chat options
- [ ] Mobile responsive
